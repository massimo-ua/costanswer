Quick start

  • Step 1. Choose time mode (Forecast or Actual) by pressing the appropriate button
  • Step 2. Fill blank cells of “Project settings” with data required
  • Step 3. You can skip entering any cost item by using “0” values
  • Step 4. Press the “Fixed Manufacturing Overhead” button
  • Step 5. Fill blank cells of FMOH “settings” with data required
  • Step 6. If your FMOH calculation is based on “Full cost applied” approach, you must fill blank cells of each type of FMOH with the values required
  • Step 7. Press the “FMOH report” button to see the annual fixed manufacturing overhead report
  • Step 8. Choose cost method by pressing the appropriate button
  • Step 9. Press the “Create product or service” button and fill blank cells of every costing element with the values required
  • Step 10. Press the “Markup” button to make the proper product or service pricing
  • Step 11. Press the “Report” button to see the product’s annual cost and budget report
  • Step 12. Press the “Project report” button to see project’s annual budget report
