Quick start

  1. Choose time mode (Forecast or Actual) by pressing the appropriate button.
  2. Fill blank cells of "Project settings" with data required.
  3. You can skip data entering of any costing element by putting "0" values.
  4. Press on "Fixed Manufacturing Overhead" button.
  5. Fill blank cells of FMOH "settings" with data required.
  6. If your FMOH calculation is based on "Full cost applied" approach, you have to fill blank cells of each type of FMOH with the values required.
  7. Press on "FMOH report" button to see annual fixed manufacturing overhead report.
  8. Choose costing method by pressing the appropriate button.
  9. Press on "Create product or service" button and fill blank cells of every costing element with the values required.
  10. Press on "Markup" button to make the proper product (service) pricing.
  11. Press on "Report" button to see the product’s annual costing and budgeting report.
  12. Press on "Project report" button to see project’s annual budgeting report.

Always follow the green colored buttons from the top to the bottom to navigate your current element selection.
