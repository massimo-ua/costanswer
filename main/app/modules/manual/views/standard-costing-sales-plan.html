Sales plan

This element reflects the volume of finished goods or services to be sold during each accounting period. These values do not have direct impact on the cost of the product but have a direct relation to the financial results of your project via section “Project revenue” of project’s Income statement report.

Data input

Please, enter quantity of finished goods or services to be sold during each accounting period.

When you are done with values, please, press button “Save”.

You can introduce changes to saved element simply by clicking on it and, after the changes are done, press button “Update”.

Important note: please, keep in mind, that you have to take into account the volume of finished goods beginning at the beginning of each accounting period and volume of finished goods ending at the end of each accounting period. The mathematical rule is simple – you cannot sell more than you have in stock at the beginning of the accounting period. In case you break this rule the system will show you the warning message.
