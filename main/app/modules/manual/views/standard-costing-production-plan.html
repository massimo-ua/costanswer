Production plan

This item reflects the number of finished goods to be produced or services to be delivered during each accounting period. These values have a direct impact on the product cost and are very important for the project calculations.

Data entry fields:

Please, enter the quantity of finished goods to be produced or services to be delivered during each accounting period (month).

When you are done entering this amount, please, press the button “Save” button.

You can change a saved item by clicking on it. After the changes are done, press the “Update” button.

Important note: Keep in mind that during your production output forecast you have to take into account the volume of finished goods already available in the warehouse inventory at the beginning of each accounting period.
