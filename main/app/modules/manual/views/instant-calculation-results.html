Instant calculation results

You can analyze the instant calculation results of the element after you click on its button and its color changes to green. COSTANSWER allows to see different parts of total product (service) costing report during your data input due to simultaneous connection to our servers. That can help you to evaluate element impact either on specific sections of the report or the project as the whole. If you see the necessity to introduce changes to the element data you can instantly correct your numbers or text.

Instant calculation results represent slightly different data for the fixed manufacturing overhead (FMOH) and Product/Service elements.

For the FMOH elements instant calculation results show summary of all elements of the section plus total FMOH:

For the Product/Service elements instant calculation results show the cost per batch of each element plus total summary of the section:

If you made a decision to create one more element during instant calculation results observations, simply press button “Update” and you will be redirected to the empty input form of the new element.
