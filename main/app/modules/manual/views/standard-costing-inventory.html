Product inventory

This item reflects the quantity of finished goods available in your storage or warehouse at the beginning of the accounting period – the first month of your project. This amount does not have direct impact on the cost of the product.

It refers to company's current inventory assets valuation in the Cost of Goods Manufactured (COGM) schedule. Inventory assets are: WIP, raw materials and finished goods. The COGM schedule is a very helpful managerial tool. It reports and adjusts the total manufacturing costs for the accounting period (month, year) in the work‐in‐process inventory account to calculate the cost of goods manufactured.

The amount of the COGM is transferred to the finished goods inventory account during the accounting period and is used to calculate the cost of goods sold on the Income Statement Report. When comparing the accounting of several Income Statement Reports over time, you can track the changes of your gross profit volumes. This helps you set future goals and strategies for sales, inventory and overhead.

Screen 1

Data entry fields:

Please enter the quantity of finished products available in your storage or warehouse at the beginning of the first month of the project.

When you are done entering this amount, please, press the button “Save” button.

You can change a saved item by clicking on it. After the changes are done, press the “Update” button.

Important note: Keep in mind that service is intangible, so it cannot be stored. That is why it is impossible to have its inventory value at the beginning of the accounting period.
