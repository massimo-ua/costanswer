Product/Service creation

After pressing “Standard costing” button, its color changes to green and you will see an empty “Product/Service settings” form for your first product or service.

Screen 1

Data entry fields:

Only “Product/Service name” and “Measurement unit” data entry fields are mandatory. Please, pay special attention to the data field “Standard quantity per:” as this is where you must choose the type of batch in which you are going to make your further computations. There are 2 options available – “per month” or “per batch” approach.

Screen 2

What is the difference? It’s just a matter of choice and your cost methodology.

Some users are ready to enter the numbers for the standard volume of assigned direct materials and applied direct labor hours required to manufacture a standard monthly quantity of the product. Other users feel more comfortable entering the numbers for standard volume of assigned direct materials and applied direct labor hours required to manufacture a standard number of products per certain batch.

For example, you know that you need 800 square feet of denim to manufacture a batch of 45 jeans of XL size. It’s a standard quantity of denim required and it takes 2 days to make that quantity (batch) of jeans. In total, you plan to manufacture 1400 pairs of jeans during the first month of the year.

In both cases it means that your product can be evaluated as a standard quantity of units (per batch or per month) at entering-the-data stage corresponding to the standard volume of purchased and assigned raw materials, applied labor hours and manufacturing overhead.

After pressing the “Save” button you will create the new product or service. It will be automatically recorded and stored on our secured cloud-based server as part of your current project. The button with the product name is the visual representation of each created product or service.

You can change a saved item by clicking on it. After the changes are done, press the “Update” button.

You can delete product or service from the project calculations by pressing “X”.
