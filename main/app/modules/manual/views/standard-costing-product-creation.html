Product/Service creation

After pressing “Standard costing” button, its color changes to green and you will see empty “Product/Service settings” input form of your first product or service.

Only “Product/Service name” and “Measurement unit” input fields are mandatory for data to be entered.

Please, pay special attention to the input field “Standard quantity per:” where you have to choose the type of batch you are going to make your further computations. There are 2 options available – “per month” or “per batch” approach.

What is the difference? It’s just a matter of your convenience and your costing methodology.

Some users are ready to enter the standard volume of assigned direct materials and applied direct labor hours required to manufacture a standard monthly quantity of the product. Other users feel more comfortable to operate with the standard volume of assigned direct materials and applied direct labor hours required to manufacture a standard number of products per certain batch.

For example, you know that you need 800 square feet of denim to manufacture a batch of 45 jeans. It’s a standard quantity of denim required and it takes 2 days to make that quantity (batch) of jeans. All together you plan to manufacture 675 pairs of jeans during the first month of the year:

If you choose “per month” approach you should know that in order to manufacture 675 pairs of jeans during the first month you need 12000 square feet of denim. It’s a standard quantity of denim required. And it takes a month to make that number of jeans:

In both cases it means that your product can be evaluated as a standard quantity of units (per batch or per month) at entering-the-data stage corresponding to the standard volume of purchased and assigned raw materials, applied labor hours and manufacturing overhead.

After pressing the button “Save” you will create the new product or service. It will be automatically recorded and stored on our secured cloud-based server as part of your current project. The button with the name is the visual expression of each created product or service.

You can introduce changes to saved element simply by clicking on it and, after the changes are done, press button “Update”.

You can delete product or service from the project calculations by pressing “X” on the button.
