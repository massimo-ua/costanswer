How to Read Our Reports

COSTANSWER generates several types of reports: fixed manufacturing overhead report, product/service report and project report. Every project can be saved in 2 formats – “pdf” and “csv.”

Fixed manufacturing overhead report

This report shows the calculation results of each FMOH item per period as well as the total FMOH summary result.

Screen 1

Product/service report

This report gives the answers to the key cost accounting and budgeting questions of each product manufactured or service delivered:

  • How does batch quantity of raw material required change in different periods?
  • How does purchasing price per raw material change in different periods?
  • How does safety stock level impact the cost of raw material at the end of the accounting period?
  • What is the cost of raw material purchase in different periods?
  • What quantity of raw material do you need to purchase in different periods?
  • What is the cost of raw material assigned to finished goods manufactured in different periods?
  • What quantity of raw material is assigned to finished goods manufactured in different periods?

Screen 2

  • What is the cost of direct labor and amount of payroll taxes in different periods?

Screen 3

  • What is the cost of variable manufacturing overhead in different periods?

Screen 4

  • What is the cost per unit, the cost per batch, and the selling price in different periods?

Project report

This report gives the answers to the key cost accounting and budgeting questions of the total project:

  • What is the total manufacturing cost?
  • What is the total cost of variable manufacturing overhead?
  • What is the total cost of fixed manufacturing overhead?
  • What is the total cost of direct materials?
  • What is the total cost of direct labor?
