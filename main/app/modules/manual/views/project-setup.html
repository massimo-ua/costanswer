Project setup

Project’s setup is simple and easy to perform. The key task is to set the time frames of your project’s duration.

Data input

“Currency” input field corresponds to the currency of your project’ financial values. Please, choose your country’s currency from the selective menu:

“Company name” input field corresponds to the name of your organization. All your projects are securely protected and stored in your personal cabinet on our cloud servers.

Sometimes confidential data about your business as the name of your company does not need to be inputted, if you prefer not to. For that purpose you can use the general names like “ABC company” or “My company” to enter this field.

Every project can be saved in 2 formats – “pdf” and “csv”. If you need to send the final project report to your friends or partners or print it for the visual presentation, “csv” format allows you to change the company name for the real one after you open it on your computer:

“Year” input field corresponds to the year when your project starts:

“Month” input field corresponds to the month when your project starts. Every project’s duration is 12 months (a year):
