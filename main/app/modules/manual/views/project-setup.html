Project Setup

Project’s setup is simple and easy to perform. The key task is to set the time frames of your project’s duration.

Data Entry Fields:

“Currency” field is the currency of your project’s financial values. Please, choose your country’s currency from the selective menu.

Screen 1

“Company name” field is the name of your organization. All of your projects are securely protected and stored in your personal cabinet on our cloud-based servers.

If you’d rather not, confidential data about your business, such as the name of your company does not need to be entered. For that purpose, you can use the general names like “ABC company” or “My company” to fill this field.

Every project can be saved in 2 formats – “pdf” and “csv.” If you need to send the final project report to your friends or partners or print it for the visual presentation, “csv” format allows you to change the company name for the real one after you open it on your computer.

Screen 2

“Year” field is the year when your project starts.

“Month” field is the month when your project starts. Every project’s duration is 12 months (a year).

When you are done with names and dates, please, press the “Next” button.

You can change saved project settings by clicking on “Project settings” button. After changes are done, press the “Next” button.

Screen 3

The new project can be saved by clicking “Save” button.

Screen 4

The existing project can be saved as a new version with new name by clicking the “Duplicate” button. It’s a time saving option if you want to create a different version of the same project because you don’t need to enter the same data again.
