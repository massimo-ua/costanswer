Variable manufacturing overhead

Manufacturing overhead can be broken into 2 types – fixed manufacturing overhead and variable manufacturing overhead.

Fixed manufacturing overhead costs occur in every accounting period regardless of the number of products you manufacture.

Variable manufacturing overhead depends strictly on the number of units produced, and reflects some costs that can be entirely assigned to the total batch of product or service – for example the cost of batch transportation, batch customs duties, batch shipment cost, the cost of electricity to produce a batch, water, lubricants or outsourced services.
