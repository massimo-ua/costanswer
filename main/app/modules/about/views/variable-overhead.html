Variable manufacturing overhead

Manufacturing overhead can be broken into 2 types – fixed manufacturing overhead and variable manufacturing overhead.

Fixed manufacturing overhead costs incur every accounting period regardless of product quantities you manufacture.

Variable manufacturing overhead strictly depends on the number of units produced and reflects some costs that can be entirely assigned to the total batch of product or service – for example the cost of batch transportation, batch custom duties, batch shipment cost, the cost of electricity to produce a batch, water, lubricates or, for example, some outsourced service or cost.
