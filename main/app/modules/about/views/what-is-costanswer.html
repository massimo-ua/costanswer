What is COSTANSWER?

COSTANSWER is a decision-making tool. It is a unique and flexible helper for most of your needs in costing and budgeting. Using our software you can:

  • Make costing calculations for an unlimited number of products or services and their components â€“ direct materials, direct labor and manufacturing overhead
  • Use the 3 most widely used costing methods simultaneously, for your accounting period, depending on your type of business or the type of customer orders you receive
  • Create an annual manufacturing plan (forecast) to project all your expenditures on direct materials, labor and manufacturing overheads
  • Arrange monthly monitoring of actual expenditures on direct materials, labor and manufacturing overheads
  • Compare plans with actual results using variance analysis
  • Model up to 5 different bases for manufacturing overhead allocation
  • Find out the values of predetermined overhead rates to simplify your forecasting procedure
  • Calculate the correct mark-up of each product or service, to make your business profitable and competitive at the same time
  • Make sales assumptions or trace actual sales results for each product or service to analyze the total volume of your revenue, cost of goods sold and gross profit
  • Produce a detailed professional cost analysis report for each product or service, total sales and manufacturing plans

COSTANSWER is not an ERP or an accounting program. At least for the moment.
