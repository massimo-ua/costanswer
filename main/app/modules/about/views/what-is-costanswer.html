What is COSTANSWER?

COSTANSWER is a decision-making tool. It is a unique and flexible helper for most of your needs in costing and budgeting. Using our software you can:

  • Make costing calculations for unlimited number of products or services and their components â€“ direct materials, direct labor and manufacturing overhead
  • Use 3 most widely used costing methods simultaneously, for the accounting period, depending on your type of business or the type of customer orders you receive.
  • Create an annual manufacturing plan (forecast) to project all your expenditures of direct materials, labor and manufacturing overheads.
  • Arrange monthly monitoring of actual expenditures of direct materials, labor and manufacturing overheads.
  • Compare forecasted plans with actual results using variance analysis
  • Model 5 different bases for manufacturing overhead allocation
  • Find out the values of predetermined overhead rates to simplify your forecasting procedure
  • Make correct mark-up of each product or service to make your business profitable and competitive at the same time.
  • Make sales assumptions or trace actual sales results for each product or service to analyze the total vulume of your revenue, cost of goods sold and gross profit.
  • Produce a detailed professional cost analysis report for each product or service, total sales and manufacturing plans.

COSTANSWER is not an ERP or an accounting program. At least for the moment.
