Direct vs indirect

In relation to cost units, costs can be either Direct or Indirect depending on the type of participation in the creation of a product or service. Direct costs include the labor cost directly associated with a product or service, the cost of raw materials and cost of utilities like water or electricity. Indirect costs include mostly overhead expenses.

To simplify user data input, COSTANSWER groups all indirect materials and labor costs in the fixed manufacturing overhead (FMOH) directory.
